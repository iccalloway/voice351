Introduction

The Voice Translator imitates human speech. More precisely, it solves an analogy problem: what is to Danny saying "colorless green ideas" as Sarah saying "the quick brown fox" is to Danny saying "he quick brown fox"? The answer will be an audio signal that sounds like Sarah saying "colorless green ideas".
